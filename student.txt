<td><I>Blooket</I> takes review and makes it fun. This site combines trivia and games. Explore the library of pre-made content, or create your own assignment. Players answer questions to help them win games. Students can play in a live game mode versus their classmates or in a solo mode for review on their own time.</td>
<td><I>Nearpod</I> makes lessons interactive through videos, games, activities, and more. Not only does this tool increase student engagement, but it also provides real-time data to teachers. Lessons can be set to teacher-led or student-paced. <I>Nearpod</I> also has a library of pre-made lessons, or you can create your own.</td>
<td>Created by the <I>SMART Technologies</I> company, <I>Lumio</I> is a digital learning tool that makes lessons collaborative and engaging. You can upload your own lessons or choose from a variety of lessons uploaded by other teachers. <I>Lumio</I> lessons can also be teacher-led for whole-group learning, or student-paced for individual practice.</td>
<td><I>UNICEF Kid Power</I> is a great resource for brain breaks in the classroom. As you complete videos, you earn points as a class that unlocks food packets for kids in need around the world. The videos on this site encompass several topics ranging from mental health to physical activity and social/emotional well-being.</td>
<td><I>Canva</I> is a graphic design tool with pre-made templates that can be customized to your liking. You can use it to create social media posts, presentations, videos, and more. This tool is a great way to allow students to showcase their learning in a variety of different ways, versus a traditional slides presentation.</td>
